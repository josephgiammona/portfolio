About Me

Joseph Giammona is an aspiring Full-Stack Programmer from Long Island, New York. Joseph started his journey in coding while studying Music Education in SUNY Potsdam. This is where he was introduced to his first programming language: RTCmix in his first Electronic Music class. This opened up his interest in computer programming and using code to be creative.

Joseph took his biggest step into the coding world after being introdcuted to Codecademy through another friend that was starting their coding journey at the time.
